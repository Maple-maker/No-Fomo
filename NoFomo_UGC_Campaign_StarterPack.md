# NoFomo — UGC Campaign Starter Pack (3 Videos)

**App positioning:** NoFomo surfaces early signals, trends, and momentum — combined with AI analysis — to spot catalysts for upside and *mispriced-future* opportunities before the crowd. The brand promise is in the name: stop watching moves happen to other people.

**Platforms (this batch):** TikTok · YouTube Shorts · X (Twitter)
**Formats (this batch):** Screen-record + AI voiceover, and "Founder POV" lifestyle
**Tool split:** *Claude* writes the analysis + scripts + on-screen text + captions. *Higgsfield* generates the talking avatar / b-roll / motion. You stitch + post.

> **Compliance note (read once, applies to everything below):** All three concepts use *real* companies and *real* recent events as illustrative examples of the kind of signal NoFomo surfaces — not as buy/sell recommendations. Every script ends with a "not financial advice — do your own research" line, and none of them promise returns or claim the app predicted a price move. Keep that discipline: NoFomo *shows you the signal*; the viewer decides. This keeps you safe with TikTok/Meta/X financial-promotion policies and with the SEC/FINRA spirit around finfluencer content.

1. **Pick the signal (Claude):** ask for "3 real companies this week with a near-term catalyst, a mispriced-future story, and a risk/sentiment warning — with sources." Verify dates/numbers before filming.
2. **Draft the script (Claude):** request hook ×3 + 30–40s script + on-screen text + captions per platform + disclaimer. Keep the "show the signal, viewer decides" framing.
